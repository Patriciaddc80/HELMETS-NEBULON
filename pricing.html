Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the warranty?

Our warranty covers manufacturing defects and includes free repairs or replacement of defective parts. The duration varies by plan: Basic (1 year), Premium (3 years), and Pro (Lifetime).

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The price difference will be calculated based on the remaining warranty period of your current plan.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders. Please contact our sales team for more information about bulk purchase options.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfers. For bulk orders, we can also arrange custom payment terms.
